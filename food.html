Food Conservation

Preserving and consuming food sustainably is crucial to reduce waste and ensure global food security. Food and Agriculture Organizations reports that 1.3 billion tons of food are wasted annually, contributing to 8% of global greenhouse gases.

Actions to Preserve and Consume Food

  • Plan meals to avoid overbuying and reduce waste by 20-30%.
  • Store food properly (e.g., refrigeration) to extend shelf life.
  • Compost leftovers and donate excess to food banks.
  • Choose local, seasonal produce to lower transport emissions.
