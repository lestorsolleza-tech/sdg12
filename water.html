Water Conservation

Conserving water is essential to prevent scarcity and protect ecosystems. The UN reports that 2.2 billion people lack safely managed drinking water, and 80% of wastewater is untreated.

Actions to Preserve and Conserve Water

  • Fix leaks and use water-efficient fixtures to reduce household use by 15-20%.
  • Collect rainwater for gardening and non-drinking purposes.
  • Adopt sustainable agriculture practices like drip irrigation.
  • Recycle wastewater in industries and promote awareness campaigns.
